I am a master student at EPFL originally from China. I study machine learning and apply those techniques in brain sciences research. I just finished my bachelor thesis with Herbert Jaeger and Benn Godde on EEG classification using Echo State Neural Network at Jacobs University. Right now I am doing a summer internship at Max Planck Tuebingen for Intelligent Systems group with Dr. Moritz Grosse-Wentrup on transfer learning of brain-computer-interfaces.

I strive to understand some important aspects about the brain and think about how to use brain sciences studies to create real-world applications especially in the areas of brain-computer-interfaces and human-like intelligence.

I strongly believe that in the coming decades, the fundamental research from neuroscience can be readily applicable to many much-needed applications that can change the lives of many. Therefore, I plan to launch my own company eventually to facilitate this upcoming technological revolution.
